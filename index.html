About

BALI KING CRAFTS is a creative studio located in Gianyar, Bali, Indonesia, specializing in the artistry and uniqueness of antique ironware. Our studio is committed to providing services that showcase the inherent beauty and strength in each creation. From captivating wrought iron artworks to the unique touch in every antique item we offer, BALI KING CRAFTS is the perfect place to discover the timeless charm of historical treasures crafted from iron. Explore the blend of creativity and craftsmanship at BALI KING CRAFTS, where each creation tells a story of history, art, and enduring beauty. We accept designs from buyers, and we are sure we can make them. Remember, good quality crafts, BALI KING CRAFTS is the solution
